      About

      Infant Cry Analyzer is a parent-friendly web application that connects to your existing Flask backend and Kaggle-trained machine learning models for infant cry analysis.

      It is designed only for infants below one year of age. Outputs are probabilistic and depend on recording quality.

      This is not a replacement for medical advice, diagnosis, or emergency care. Always consult your pediatrician or local emergency services when you are concerned about your baby.

      More: · ·

      How it works

      1. Capture sound — Upload or record a short cry clip.
      2. Audio check — The server distinguishes baby cry, adult speech, noise, unclear audio, or silence.
      3. Risk layer — Your existing RF model scores asphyxia-related risk with the same thresholds and features.
      4. Care hints — If risk is below threshold, you may see a possible everyday cause (heuristic) plus guidance.

      Emergency guidance

      Call your local emergency number if your baby is struggling to breathe, turning blue or gray, is unresponsive, has a seizure, or you believe their life is at risk.

      Seek urgent medical advice for persistent high fever in a young infant, poor feeding with dehydration signs, repeated vomiting, blood in stool, or inconsolable crying that is very unlike usual—especially with lethargy or a bulging fontanelle.

      This app cannot detect all emergencies. Trust your instincts and your clinician.

      Contact / Help

      For deployment or technical issues, contact your maintainer. For health concerns about your child, use your pediatrician or nurse line—not this tool.
